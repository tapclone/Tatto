About Us

By putting the customer first each time they come in and making them feel unique, we at Torque Performance Parts, a break from your normal tattoo parlour, offer professional expertise and quality service to our clients. We are located in Edapally, Ernakulam, Kerala. We offer a welcoming environment for people who seek body piercings and bespoke tattoo designs in a tidy and cosy setting. Our constant goal is to satisfy the creative and aesthetic needs of our customers with quality and elegance that exceed their expectations.

It is the true fervour of a bunch of tattoo enthusiasts that paved way to the inception of this remarkable venture. Over the last Ten years, Tattoo Art Studio has become the flagbearers of an exotic tattoo culture in city centre of the queen of Arabian sea. The tattoo and piercing experts that have been employed here are the best in town and they altogether have amassed experience of decades up their sleeves. Greatly professional inking and personalized styles implemented by our artists have made Tattoo Art studio a one-stop destination for tattoo and piercing aficionados.
