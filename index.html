Tattooing And Piercing Services

3D Styles

We make 3D tattoos to capture your imagination, creating depth and optical illusion that the image is on your skin.

Solid Black Work

We make abstract patterns, and shapes, as well as figurative forms with black ink rendered on the skin.

Realism

We design Realism or Photorealism tattoos to resemble real photographs or 3D objects in your skin.

Geometric Patterns & Mandals

We design geometric symbols and pattern representations of deities, including the god and goddesses. These patterns are often portrayed in South Asian cultures.

Touch-Ups

In touch-up we repaint your tattoo to make it look more attractive or current.

Tattoo design
